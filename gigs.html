The Band

David Alt, the composer and saxophonist of Alt Tal, also directs the chamber Jazz ensemble Mutual Admiration Org, performs with Theloni On Wry, and has studied at the Pierre Monteux School for Conductors and Orchestra Musicians. Kenny Annis digs the upright bass. Kenny also performs Hindustani Classical music, on sarod, and has studied with Maestro Sharon Roni, Ustad Ali Akbar Khan, and is currently a student of Maestro Steve Oda.

Kenny is Musical Director for Bay area performance artist Vinsantos, has composed music for the Printz Dance Project, and plays bass for Damon & the Heathens.

Andrew Ryan, Drums. Andrew also performs Bata with Michael Spiro, at Rhythm Motion Dance Center and other rituals.

Along with Kenny, Andy comprises the rest of the rhythm section for Damon & the Heathens
